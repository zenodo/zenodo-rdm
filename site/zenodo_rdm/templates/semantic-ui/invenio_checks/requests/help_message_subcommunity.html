What is this about?

To ensure communities are managed by legitimate project participants, we verify that at least one community member (owner or manager) is affiliated with one of the project's partner organizations.

How do we verify affiliation?

We check the member's email domain against the project's partner organizations, or verify through connected institutional accounts (e.g., via your institution's login).

{% elif check_id == "subcommunity_validation" %}
What is this about?

We validate that the EU project grant exists and is currently active, and ensure no duplicate community has already been created for this project.

Why is this important?

This prevents creation of communities for non-existent or expired projects, and avoids confusion from having multiple communities for the same project.

{% elif check_id == "subcommunity_record_metadata" %}
What is this about?

To be part of the EU Open Research Repository, all records in a community must acknowledge their EU funding by including the project's grant information in their metadata.

How do I fix this?

You can use the "Add funding to all records" button to automatically add the grant information, or manually edit individual records. Records being edited by others will need to be updated separately.
