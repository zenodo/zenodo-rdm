Submit your research

What?

Scope:
  • Any discipline.
  • Any research output (data, software, posters, presentations, deliverables, ...).
  • For preprints and papers please consider using Open Research Europe (see below).
Requirements:
  • Research output must have been (co-)funded by the European Commission via Horizon Europe (including ERC, MCSA, ERA-NET, COST and earlier Framework Programmes) and/or Euratom.

Why?

Open science

Share and preserve any research output from your EU-funded projects.

Compliance

EUREPO ensures compliance with the Horizon Europe grant agreement which requires beneficiaries to deposit their research data in a trusted repository as open as possible as closed as necessary.

200GB

You get a default quota of 200GB instead of 50GB for your uploads.

The right data repository?

Before submitting research data please consider using discipline-specific repositories when available. Discipline-specific repositories provide tailored services and better curation. See a list of repositories for specific data types or search for one on re3data.org.

Which EU platform should I use?

Open Research Europe (ORE)

Open Research Europe (ORE) is an open access publishing venue for European Commission-funded researchers across all disciplines, with no author fees.

  • ORE helps you publish your preprints and papers.
  • ORE is peer-reviewed.
Go to Open Research Europe

vs

EU Open Research Repository

EU Open Research Repository (EUREPO) is a repository for European Commission-funded research across all disciplines.

  • EUREPO helps you publish any research output (data, software, deliverables, posters, presentations, ...).
  • EUREPO is curated (but not peer-reviewed).
